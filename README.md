# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes marketing funnel performance using the Olist Marketing Qualified Leads dataset. The objective is to identify lead generation trends, channel performance, and top-performing landing pages.

## Dataset

* Dataset: olist_marketing_qualified_leads_dataset.csv
* Records: 8000 leads

## Key Metrics

* Total Leads: 8000
* Total Channels: 10
* Total Landing Pages: 495

## Dashboard Components

* Leads by Marketing Channel
* Lead Distribution by Channel
* Lead Generation Trend
* Top Landing Pages
* Key Insights

## Key Insights

1. Organic and direct marketing channels generated a significant share of leads.
2. Lead generation varied over time, showing periods of higher acquisition activity.
3. A small number of landing pages attracted a large proportion of leads.
4. Marketing performance is concentrated in a few high-performing channels and landing pages.
5. Optimizing top-performing landing pages may improve overall lead acquisition.
